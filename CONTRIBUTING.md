# Contributing to Plutus Cheatsheet Generator

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Plutus Cheatsheet Generator.
All contributions are welcome and appreciated.

---

## Ways to Contribute

You can contribute by:
- Improving documentation
- Fixing bugs or UI issues
- Adding or improving Plutus snippets
- Reporting issues or suggesting enhancements

---
